About Us

The basketball club, founded on September 1, 2023, is a vibrant community for basketball enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Its mission is to promote teamwork, fitness, and the love of the game while fostering a supportive and inclusive environment. The club organizes regular training sessions, friendly matches, and tournaments, offering members the opportunity to enhance their skills and connect with others who share their passion for basketball.

Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting, the club welcomes everyone to be part of this exciting journey, celebrating the spirit of the sport both on and off the court.
